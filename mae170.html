MAE 170: Signal Processing Techniques

Labratory class on measurement sensors using oscilloscopes, wave form generators, and other data aquisition methods.
Each experiment relates to different kind of mechanical process (e.g Heat transfer, MEMS, DAC)
Final Grade: A

Measuring Heat transfer given different convection modes.

Using a thermistor, heating elements and arduino's, data on temperature change was aquired and depicted using Matlab.

Four cases of 2 materials, and 2 convection modes were analysed using three trials per case. The Biot numbers and Heat transfer coefficient of each scenario was analysed using the standard error estimates, percentage deviation and other statistal methods to understand the impact of each mode.

The pitfalls and successes of the experiment was highlights in the results and discussion section, with potential sources of error.

MATLAB plots

In order to visually detect aliasing, and directly compare signals sampled at different rates, MATLAB plots were scripted to indicate noise and other phenomena.

Weight-driven clock using the small angle approximation

This was an experiment to measure sound waves by constructing a jig utilizing operational amplifiers, multiple arduinos, and sound equipment. This allowed the calculation of speed of sound in the medium the device was in without prior values.
